Aggregation and plotting time series in Pandas

Tue 02 June 2015 · tagged stats

We've all grown up studying groupy by and aggregations in SQL. Pandas provides excellent functionality for group by and aggregations. However, for time series data, we need a bit of manipulation. In this post, I'll take a small example of weather time series data, about which I'd written a post previously. I'll show how aggregations can help us make sense of larger time series data. The weather CSV can be downloaded from here.

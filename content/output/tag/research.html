Best paper awards at Buildsys

Published: Wed 19 February 2014
By nipunbatra

In Blog.

tags: buildings research

In this post I enlist the papers awarded the best paper, poster and student paper awards at Buildsys.  Buildsys is a workshop held in conjunction with SenSys.

For 2011-2013, the information is available on the official Buildsys website. For the remaining, I googled to get the information.

2013

A Scalable Low-Cost Solution to Provide Personalised Home Heating Advice to Households

Optimal Personal Comfort Management Using SPOT

2012

Building Application Stack (BAS)

MLE+: A Tool for Integrated Design and Deployment of Energy Efficient Building Controls

2011

Co-simulation Based Building Controls Implementation with Networked Sensors and Actuators

Appliance Classification and Energy Management Using Multi-Modal Sensing

2010

Contactless Sensing of Appliance State Transitions

2009

Using Circuit-Level Power Measurements in Household Energy Management Systems

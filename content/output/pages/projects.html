Energy Disaggregation

The aim of energy disaggregation (or Non-intrusive load monitoring (NILM)) is to break down the household energy measured at a single point into constituent appliances. The following image demonstrates the same. Such disaggregated feedback can greatly help the consumers to optimize their consumption.

If you would like to play around, try this minimal dashboard app: http://energy.iiitd.edu.in:5002/visualize (For now just click on View data without changing any options)

Feeling the need for an end to end pipeline for such problems, along with Jack and Oliver, I have been working on a NILM toolkit [Github page][Documentation

Previously, I had worked on simplifying NILM via load division.[Publication] [Github page] [IPython notebook]

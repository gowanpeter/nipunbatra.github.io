An interesting question-Where are the pigeons coming from?

Published: Sat 08 October 2011
By nipunbatra

In Blog.

tags: algorithms

Assume that you are getting a continuous stream of data (ascii characters). How best can you tell that there is a repetition in it.

Think........
................

When i first heard it, i thought of making a hashmap, since only 128 such characters exist. And in O(n) i'll be able to report a repetition. The easier answer: The moment the length of stream is greater than 128,there is a repetition. Pigeonhole principle!

Moved to Pelican

Sun 20 April 2014 · tagged pelican

I have decided to move my blog and website to Pelican. Some of the reasons why I wanted to do this:

  • Ability to blog using IPython notebooks (N.B. This post is written as an IPython notebook)
  • Ability to run JS (Wordpress does not allow this for a variety of reasons)
  • Have better control over the entire lifecycle
